## Summary

The CRM SaaS architecture is designed for:

- **Scalability** - Horizontal scaling with load balancing
- **Multi-tenancy** - Complete data isolation per tenant
- **Security** - JWT auth, RBAC, DataScope enforcement
- **Reliability** - Health checks, retry logic, background jobs
- **Maintainability** - Clean architecture, DI, separation of concerns
- **Extensibility** - Workflow engine, webhooks, plugin architecture
